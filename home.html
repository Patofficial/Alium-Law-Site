A CLIENT-CENTRIC FIRM, ACTING IN PARTNERSHIP WITH CLIENTS WHILE TAKING A COMMERCIAL, PRACTICAL AND HOLISTIC APPROACH TO LEGAL ISSUES.

ALIUM LAW has a different approach to the practice of law.

in 2010, a group of 3 legal professionals started “Kibungei & Onyango”, which later became “ALIUM LAW”. Our goal was to create a 21st Century law firm that would always provide a better, faster, higher value legal product to sophisticated purchasers of legal services. The firm prides itself on providing excellent services comparable to any of the more established firms due to the wide experience gained by the firm’s Partners. Seeking to develop a new approach to the provision of legal services, the firm leverages on technology to serve the needs of clients. The intention behind this was and remains to provide a range and quality of services to be expected from a leading commercial city practice, but with emphasis on developing a personal relationship between an advocate and clients.

With offices strategically located near the quiet and serene Westlands area along Waiyaki Way in Nairobi, the firm is well placed to serve the legal needs of clients of all sizes. The Firm has a vision of being and remaining a top-notch law firm specializing in commercial law practice in Kenya.

ALIUM LAW is primarily known for its Commercial, Real Estate, Banking, Intellectual Property and Employment & Immigration law practice.

We are committed to understanding the needs of each of our clients and being real partners in their business and growth.
