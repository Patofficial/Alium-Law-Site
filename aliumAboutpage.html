ABOUT US

We take a practical and holistic approach to legal issues

OUR DIFFERENCE:

Alium Law has a different approach to the practice of law. We are committed to understanding the needs of each of our clients and being real partners in their business and growth. We have built a client-centric firm, leveraging technology and adaptable enough to serve the needs of clients in a transforming world.

Technology

Our technology helps us serve you better by improving how we deliver our services and collaborate together. You need advisors who are accessible and easy to work with, so we have assembled the best tools to communicate and collaborate together in a way that is convenient for you.

Communication

If physical “in-person” meetings are not possible for the client, we can exchange secure messages online or video conference and review documents together on-screen while doing so. We also provide our clients the option of using our Client Portal to exchange secure messages instead.

Collaboration

With our Client Portal, our clients get access to their legal documents through a web-based Extranet. Your legal documents are at your fingertips whenever you need them. For major projects, we can setup portal access to shared calendar events, tasks, and account information. We provide the height of transparency and accessibility.

Experience

What good is a world of experience if you are stuck doing things the same way? In 2010, a group of 3 legal professionals started “Kibungei & Onyango”, which later became “Alium Law”. Our goal was to create a 21st Century law firm that would always provide a better, faster, higher value legal product to sophisticated purchasers of legal services. The firm prides itself on providing excellent services comparable to any other firm due to the wide experience gained by the firm’s Partners. We have years of experience but we are also always adapting and getting better. We are relentless in our efforts to improve the efficiency, effectiveness and flexibility of our services (and our people and processes too).
